My Story

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm Spandan Banerjee, a passionate Software Developer and Data Science Enthusiast currently working at IBM. My journey in technology began during my time at St. Thomas' College of Engineering and Technology in Kolkata, where I discovered my love for problem-solving and innovation.

What started as curiosity about how things work has evolved into a career focused on creating meaningful solutions that bridge the gap between complex data and actionable insights. I believe that technology should be accessible, impactful, and used to solve real-world problems.

My Philosophy

I approach every project with three core principles:

  • Curiosity-Driven Learning: Always asking "why" and "how can we improve this?"
  • Collaborative Innovation: The best solutions come from diverse perspectives working together
  • Impact-Focused Development: Code should solve real problems for real people
